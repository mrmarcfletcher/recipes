Beef Stew

A hearty, warming stew perfect for cold winter evenings. Tender chunks of beef slow-cooked with root vegetables in a rich, savory gravy.

The meat becomes incredibly tender after hours of gentle simmering, practically falling apart at the touch of your fork. The vegetables soak up all those delicious meaty flavors.

Best served with crusty bread for dipping, or spooned over creamy mashed potatoes. Your kitchen will smell amazing while this cooks.
